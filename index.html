How it works

A clear handoff keeps food safe and fast.

01

Share food details

Donor adds quantity, food type, prepared time, pickup area, and contact number.

02

Contact nearby care

The donor calls or messages the nearest old age home, orphanage, or NGO partner.

03

Confirm safe pickup

The receiver confirms capacity, packing needs, pickup person, and delivery timing.

04

Record impact

Both sides note meals received, time, and any quality feedback for better future donations.

Food safety

Donate only food that is safe to serve.

Pack cleanly

Use clean, covered containers. Keep veg and non-veg food separate and label allergens clearly.

Act quickly

Contact recipients as soon as the event ends. Share the prepared time and pickup deadline.

Avoid risk

Do not donate spoiled, reheated multiple times, uncovered, or half-eaten food.
